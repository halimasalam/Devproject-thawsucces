Thaw Success Global (TSG) Limited is a custom-licensed exporting agent company established to offer comprehensive world-class integrated services and other export business solutions within and outside Nigeria.

We specialize in all aspects of exportation of agro-commodities (Cashew nuts, sesame seeds, cocoa, Ginger, etc) and other essential commodities from Nigeria to any part of the world.

Alongside our various services, the core of our business is to provide the best management know-how, state-of-the-art technology, and efficient logistic followup that works for you and your company's needs.

Over the years, TSG has built a dedicated Team of Experts that work to ensure that the quality benchmarks we have set are not just on papers but are in action from procurement to packaging, and finally to shipping. In addition, our team ensures that the shipments are always on schedule. They are also equipped with the required tools and means to offer our clients the needed quality assurance checks according to their specific requirements.
